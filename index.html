Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tanya rescues the cats (the “App”) handles user data.

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or user data. All functionality operates entirely on the user’s device, with no internet access required.

2. Use of Data

Since no data is collected, the App does not use any user information for analytics, advertising, personalization, or any other purpose.

3. Sharing with Third Parties

The App does not share any data with third parties—including analytics providers, advertising networks, or cloud services—because no data is ever collected.

4. Access to Data

No one, including the developer, has access to user data, as such data is neither stored nor transmitted.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated “Last updated” date.
